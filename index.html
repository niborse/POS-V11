This is an MVP (currently in the initial phase). Navigate through the following options: Click on 'Point of Sale' to start selling. The system utilizes a roboflow model to identify grocery items. At present, the model is trained to detect specific items such as Apple-Royal-Gala, Banana, Bread-Hovis, Bread-Warburtons, Melon, Milk-Arla-Gravendale-Whole-Milk, Milkybar, Walkers-Salt-and-Vinegar, and Orange. MongoDB Atlas is used to retrieve item details detected by the model , "Purchased" for purchase orders, "Dashboard" for analytics, and "Inventory" to manage stock.
